Puffpuff Recipe

Puffpuff

Is a deep-fried dough. They are made from yeast dough, shaped into balls and deep-fried until golden brown. It has a doughnut-like texture but slightly more chewy. It is a very common snack eaten by so many countries but with different names and a little bit of variation in the mode of preparation.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Warm water
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Cooking oil

Preparation

  1. Activate the yeast: Mix salt, water, sugar, and yeast. Set it aside for 5 minutes
  2. Make the dough: Add flour and mix
  3. Rest and Rise: Let the mixture rise for approximately 1-2 hours
  4. Pre-heat the oil: In a large sauce pan, pour vegetable oil into a pot until it is about 3 inches (or 5 centimeters ) high (too little will result into a flatter balls), and place in low heat
  5. Check the heat: Test to make sure the oil is hot enough by putting a drop of batter into the oil. If the oil is not hot enough, the batter will stay at the bottom instead of rising to the top.
  6. Fry the puffpuff:Using your hands, grab a little bit of mixture at a time and drop into the oil
  7. Remove from oil: Use a large spoon or a slotted spoon to take it out of the oil. I usually place them on napkins right away to soak up some of the excess oil.
  8. Coat the puff puff: If desired, you can roll up the finished products in table sugar or powdered sugar to make it sweeter.Enjoy
