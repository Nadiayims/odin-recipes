Pile recipe

Pile de pommes Is a dish from the West of Cameroon made of mashed potatoes and black beans.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg peeled and washed potatoes
  • 500 g cooked red or black beans
  • 80ml palm oil
  • 1 cup (235ml) water
  • African hot pepper puree (optional) according to your spice tolerance
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Pour the potatoes into a saucepan, add water, cover and cook. When the potatoes are cooked, pour out the excess water or leave to dry in the open pan
  2. Add the beans, cover and cook for 5 minutes over low heat
  3. Add the palm oil, salt, add the chilli puree, crush everything with a pestle or a bottle. Enjoy this dish when it is hot enough
